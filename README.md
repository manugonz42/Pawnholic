> An incremental pawnshop game with dark humor — Buy junk, haggle, flip it, repeat.

## Gameplay

- 🛒 Buy items from customers at your pawnshop
- 💵 Haggle to get the best prices
- 📈 Upgrade your shop and unlock new categories
- 🎯 Deal with suspicious customers and unexpected events
- 💰 Build your pawnshop empire
